Design a full multi-page, professional website for a startup that provides paid internship opportunities to university students.
The goal of the website is to build trust, show legitimacy, and make the application process extremely simple.
Use a clean, modern, corporate UI design style with plenty of white space, structured layouts, and strong visual hierarchy.”

⸻

🎨 Overall UI Style
	•	Professional corporate tone
	•	Clean white + navy primary palette
	•	Accent color: teal or royal blue
	•	Soft shadows and subtle depth
	•	Minimalistic icons
	•	Rounded but not playful—keep it serious
	•	Clear typography hierarchy (H1 bold, body clean)
	•	Consistent spacing, grid-based structure
	•	Mobile-first responsiveness

⸻

📌 Pages to Design

⸻

1️⃣ Home Page (Landing Page)

Goal: Establish trust + guide user to “Apply for Internship.”

Sections:

Hero Section
	•	Full-width clean background
	•	Bold headline: “Secure Your Internship With Real Industry Projects”
	•	Subtext: short 1–2 lines about solving the internship shortage
	•	One primary CTA: Apply Now
	•	Secondary CTA: Browse Programs
	•	Visual: Illustration or minimal professional imagery

Internship Categories
	•	Grid of cards (IT, Marketing, Data Science, UI/UX, Cybersecurity, Finance etc.)
	•	Each card = icon + 1-line description

How It Works (3–4 step graphic)
	1.	Sign up
	2.	Choose program
	3.	Complete real projects
	4.	Get certificate

Use minimal infographic-style visuals.

Project Samples
	•	Preview cards of past projects
	•	Each card includes: title, skillset, duration, difficulty level

Pricing Section
	•	Simple transparent plans
	•	“₹3000 Internship Program”
	•	Bullet list of what’s included

Testimonials
	•	Carousel of 3–5 students
	•	Clean headshots, quotes, and university names

CTA Strip
	•	Clean banner: “Start Your Career With Real Experience”
	•	Action button

Footer
	•	Company info
	•	Contact email
	•	Social icons
	•	Privacy Policy / Terms links

⸻

2️⃣ About Us Page

Goal: Build credibility & origin story.

Sections:
	•	Founder’s story: why the company was created
	•	Mission & vision
	•	Values (professionalism, accessibility, affordability)
	•	Timeline of company journey
	•	Team section with professional portraits + titles

⸻

3️⃣ Internship Programs Page

Goal: Show all available internships clearly.

UI components:
	•	Filter bar (domain, duration, difficulty)
	•	Cards for each internship:
	•	Title
	•	Duration (3 or 6 months)
	•	What skills will be learned
	•	What tools they’ll use
	•	“Apply Now” button

⸻

4️⃣ How It Works Page

Goal: Clarify the process visually.
	•	Step-by-step illustrated flow (clean vector style):
	•	Registration
	•	Orientation
	•	Project assignment
	•	Weekly evaluations
	•	Completion report
	•	Certification
	•	FAQ section with accordions

⸻

5️⃣ Contact / Support Page
	•	Clean contact form (Name, Email, University, Message)
	•	Live chat icon placeholder
	•	Support email section

⸻

6️⃣ Payment Page (razorpay-ready layout)
	•	Order summary
	•	₹3000 fee
	•	Razorpay-style button placeholder
	•	Trust badges
	•	Refund disclaimer (professional tone)

⸻

7️⃣ Dashboard Page (Optional)

For students after login:
	•	Left sidebar nav
	•	Assigned project
	•	Weekly tasks
	•	Upload section
	•	Certificate progress bar

⸻

✨ Tone & Vibe
	•	Professional
	•	Trustworthy
	•	Modern, structured
	•	Zero clutter
	•	Clear CTAs
	•	Strong focus on credibility and transparency
